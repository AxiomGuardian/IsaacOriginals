K.I.T

Kingdom Initiative Technologies.

We are building software and systems to disciple, equip, and connect the Church with precision and order.

The vision

Technology bent toward the Church.

At its core, K.I.T exists to help believers walk with Christ daily and to help churches disciple their people effectively in a distracted world.

The enemy has used technology to disciple a generation into anxiety, comparison, and isolation. The Church doesn't need to hide from the tools — she needs saints willing to rebuild them for the glory of God and the good of His people.

K.I.T is my attempt at that. A slow, prayerful body of work — building real systems that serve real believers, rooted in Scripture and built to last. Every tool points back to the local church. Every line of code is stewardship.

"For God is not a God of disorder but of peace." — 1 Corinthians 14:40

What's being built

Current focus.

These tools are meant to support your daily walk with Christ and your local church — not replace the Holy Spirit or the local church.

Disciple

WisdomWatch

A personal discipleship journal that combines your honest thoughts with Grok-4-powered, Scripture-rooted insight and actionable next steps.

Equip

K.C.R.M.

Kingdom Church Relationship Management — a tool for pastors and leaders to disciple their people with clarity, track spiritual growth, and build real community.

These are not finished products yet. We are still in active development — building with care, prayer, and excellence so the tools are worthy of the calling.

The bigger vision

K.I.T is infrastructure for the Kingdom.

What we are building today is only the beginning. These are the directions we're praying over — whatever the Lord confirms, that's what gets built.

Connect

Nexus

The secure collaboration hub where churches and leaders connect, share resources, host joint teaching, and build relationship across distances. Anti-scroll, anti-clout, pro-presence.

Intelligence

N.I.A.

Neural Interface Architecture — future AR/VR tools that allow churches to plant, train, and disciple in immersive environments, bringing the reality of the Kingdom into physical space.

Scale

White-label

The same truth-centered systems made available for military chaplains, Christian schools, missions organizations, and other Kingdom-minded groups who want to steward technology without compromise.

Guardrails

  • A personality cult. The founder is not the point.

  • Built in secret. You'll see every brick laid in public.
