About

I'm Isaac Rodriguez.
22. Building under Christ.

For a long time I chased success, money, and what looked impressive on the outside. I failed. I got lost. I made mistakes. I ran from God more times than I can count.

Through the pain and the wandering, God has been patiently bringing me back to what matters most — simple, consistent obedience to Christ. Not perfection. Not a platform. Just faithfulness with what's in front of me today.

I'm wired to build. Christ tells me who I am — everything else is just context. Right now that means two things: ApexAERA — an intelligent marketing system that pays the bills honestly — and K.I.T — Kingdom Initiative Technologies, software built to disciple, equip, and connect the Church.

I document publicly because I've noticed two things about myself: when no one is watching, I drift; and when I pretend, I rot. This site exists to fix both. The work exists because Christ has put real problems in front of me, and there's no spiritual reason to dodge them.

I'm not a pastor. I'm not a guru. I'm not selling a system. I'm one young man trying to be obedient with what's been put in his hands. If any of this serves you — praise God. If it doesn't, also praise God.
