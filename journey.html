The Journey

Three phases.
One God through all of them.

Phase 1 2021 – 2024

Learning & Breaking

Identity formation, pain, failure, and the beginning lessons in discipline. This was the season of chasing things that didn't last — success, money, approval — and slowly realizing none of it filled the hole. God used the breaking to get my attention.

Phase 2 2025

Discipline & Return

Returning to God, rebuilding habits, and learning to steward what was given. This is where the daily rhythms started to form — prayer, work, honesty, rest. Not perfection. Just consistent, imperfect obedience.

Phase 3 2026 – Present

Execution & Building

Taking obedient action — building K.I.T, ApexAERA, and walking out the assignment with clarity and consistency. This is the season of putting the lessons to work. Still learning. Still failing forward. But now with tools in hand and a foundation under my feet.
