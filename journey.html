The Journey

The honest timeline.
Wins, losses, and the in-between.

Nothing cleaned up. Nothing retouched. Milestones only matter if the ditches in between are documented too.

Apr 2026

Build

IsaacOriginals.com goes live.

Public window opens. The plan is simple: tell the truth about the grind, point everything back to Christ, ship work that lasts.

Mar 2026

Struggle

Broke a 30-day detox streak.

Felt it coming two days out and ignored it. Got back up, confessed it, restarted the counter. No dramatic arc — just the reset.

Feb 2026

Win

Apex signs client #2.

Real contract, real delivery window. Proof that integrity scales — the work was the pitch.

Jan 2026

Faith

The K.I.T. conviction.

Walked in the desert at sunrise. Came back with a name: Kingdom Initiative Technologies. No launch yet — just a conviction worth guarding.

Dec 2025

Build

Apex's first real delivery.

Shipped the first paid AI system. Ugly code in places, honest work end-to-end. Client's workflow shaved by hours — that was the metric that mattered.

Oct 2025

Faith

Submitted the business to Christ.

Stopped treating Apex like my kingdom. Kept the work, dropped the ownership. Everything after this date belongs to Him.

Summer 2025

Origin

Started Apex from a spare room in Phoenix.

No office. No team. One laptop, one set of disciplines, one stack of books. The seed.

The point isn't the timeline. The point is that I kept going back to the Father when I had nothing left — and He kept putting breath in my lungs anyway.
