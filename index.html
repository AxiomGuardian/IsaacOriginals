The Journey About Isaac
"But seek first the kingdom of God and his righteousness, and all these things will be added to you."

I'm 22.

I have big vision, real tools, and a clear assignment from God. But I'm still learning how to walk it out without running ahead or hiding in distraction.

For years I chased success, money, and what looked good on the outside. I failed. I got lost. I made mistakes. Through the pain and the wandering, God has been steadily bringing me back to what matters most — simple, consistent obedience.

This site is my public record of that journey — the real process of a young man learning to steward what God has given him — the tools, the relationships, the opportunities, and the assignment — for His glory.

Current Focus

Two missions. One foundation.

I'm building two things right now:

K.I.T

Kingdom Initiative Technologies

Building Scripture-rooted discipleship tools for the Church. Personal journaling (WisdomWatch), church leadership systems (K.C.R.M.), and future platforms to connect believers worldwide — all grounded in absolute truth. Currently in active development.

ApexAERA

Intelligent Marketing Systems

Helping Kingdom-minded builders get clear messaging and automation so they can focus on what God called them to do. Every dollar earned through ApexAERA funds Kingdom Initiative Technologies and other God-honoring work.

This is not about building an empire for myself. It's about being faithful with what's in front of me today and documenting the process in public — so others walking the same road can be encouraged.
